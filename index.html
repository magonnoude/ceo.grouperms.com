About Me

Three decades shaping energy & digital strategy

I spent six years as Chief Digital & Information Officer for ENGIE's Africa business unit — overseeing IT across 20 countries, managing $25M+ budgets, and delivering the platforms that power B2B energy operations in emerging markets.

That was the culmination of 16 years within the ENGIE Group (2008–2024), from consultant to CDIO. Before ENGIE, I spent 12 years at Bull SA as Group CIO & Programme Director — where I built the technical foundations and leadership discipline that shaped everything that followed.

My academic path reinforces that breadth: an MBA from Ecole des Ponts Business School, a Cloud Computing degree from McCombs School of Business at UT Austin, a CTO certification from MIT, and a Master's in Computer Engineering from CNAM Paris.
