Executive Summary

I have spent over three decades building, running, and transforming technology organisations in some of the world's most demanding industries — from high-performance computing and enterprise IT to large-scale energy infrastructure across Europe and Africa.

Today, as Founder & CEO of RMS International Group, I bring that accumulated depth to boards, investors, and leadership teams who need a senior technology voice they can trust — one who understands both the boardroom and the data center.
